Dressup Demo

Here you can try out the dress-up game. It's currently in alpha testing, so there may be some bugs. Free wallpapers are by Hubble!

Instructions

Select a user and a rat from the drop-down selectors below to the right.

Click on an item in the hotbar to add it to the scene, and drag it to position.

Right click on an item to remove it from the scene.

This is just an early game demo, so there is no way to save a scene yet, but you can screenshot it to keep it.
