Valentines

There are two valentines events. Both will run concurrently. Event 1 is a literature writing contest and event 2 is a fancy dress contest. Both events will start on the 6th of February and end at midnight GMT on February the 13th, and winners will be announced on the 14th. You may enter both.

Prompt for event 1: Write stumpy a love letter
Stumpy has been feeling down in the dumps. Nobody loves her! Write her the best love poem or letter (from your rat to her) to cheer her up. Best work wins. (Judging on Pond’s discretion with stumpy’s input). You must own or borrow a rat to enter. (Remember, everyone gets a free myo custom!)
The adopt prize(s) will be awarded to the winning [b]player[/b](s), and other prizes will be awarded to your rat.

Prompt for event 2: Dress to impress Fancy dress contest! Create the best valentines themed costume. You must own or borrow a rat to enter. You [b]may[/b] draw over the adopt art of your rat or you may draw your own image from scratch. All entries will be judged on the costume idea, not the art quality! Pond and Sheb will judge jointly. The adopt prize(s) will be awarded to the winning [b]player[/b](s), and other prizes will be awarded [b]to your rat[/b].
